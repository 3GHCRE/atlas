## Why This Is a True Third-Party Sale

1. **Different Corporate Structures**
   - Omega: Public REIT (NYSE: OHI) - passive real estate investor
   - Kissito: Private regional owner-operator
   - CareTrust: Public REIT (NYSE: CTRE) - passive real estate investor
   - Larry H. Miller: Private operator

2. **Different Headquarters**
   - Omega: Hunt Valley, MD
   - Kissito: Roanoke, VA
   - CareTrust: California
   - Larry H. Miller: Utah

3. **Different Business Models**
   - Omega/CareTrust: Triple-net lease to operators (REIT)
   - Kissito: Vertically integrated owner-operator
   - Larry H. Miller: Pure operator (no real estate)

4. **Arm's Length Transaction**
   - No common ownership between parties
   - Market-rate pricing ($34.2M for 210-bed facility = ~$163K/bed)
   - Standard documentation (Special Warranty Deed)
