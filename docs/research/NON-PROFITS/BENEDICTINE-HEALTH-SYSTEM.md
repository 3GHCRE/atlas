## Executive Summary

Benedictine Health System is one of the **largest Catholic senior living organizations** in the United States, operating 35+ communities across five states. Founded in 1985 by the Sisters of St. Scholastica Monastery in Duluth, the organization has grown through acquisitions, partnerships, and sponsorship transitions.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Total Assets:** $175.8M (2023 990 filing)
- **Annual Revenue:** $84.1M (parent) + $36.8M (Living Communities) + $17.3M (Catholic Residential Services)
- **Combined Revenue:** ~$138.2M across affiliated entities
- **Debt Structure:** $132.4M obligated group financing (2021)
- **Beds/Units:** 1,815 nursing beds + 1,890 senior housing units
