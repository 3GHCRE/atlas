# Orthodox Jewish Skilled Nursing Ownership Networks

**Research Date:** January 25, 2026
**Analyst:** Atlas Database Analysis

## Executive Summary

Analysis of the Atlas SNF ownership database reveals extensive Orthodox Jewish ownership networks concentrated in the NY/NJ area, with portfolios spanning 20+ states. These networks are characterized by:

- Multiple principals serving across 10-30+ companies
- Complex PropCo/OpCo structures obscuring true ownership
- Shared back-office and management functions
- Rapid pandemic-era expansion

## Risk Indicators

### Opacity Concerns
- Multiple brand names obscure common ownership
- PropCo/OpCo separation limits liability tracking
- Management agreements vs. ownership unclear in some cases

### Financial Concentration
- Several principals with 5%+ ownership across multiple companies
- Limited diversity in decision-making
- Pandemic-era debt loads (CARES Act, PPP)
