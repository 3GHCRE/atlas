## Executive Summary

The Evangelical Lutheran Good Samaritan Society is the **largest nonprofit skilled nursing provider in the United States**, operating senior care communities across multiple states. Founded in 1922 in Arthur, North Dakota, the organization merged with **Sanford Health** on January 1, 2019, forming a combined **$5 billion nonprofit health system**.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Total Revenue:** $953.3M (2023 990 filing)
- **Total Assets:** $1.105B
- **Net Assets:** $912.3M
- **Annual Expenses:** $985.1M
- **Current Portfolio:** 64 owned/operated SNF facilities
- **Geographic Footprint:** Consolidating from 22 states to 7 core Midwest states

**MAJOR STRATEGIC SHIFT (2023-2024):** Good Samaritan announced a **massive divestiture** exiting 15 states to focus operations on South Dakota, North Dakota, Iowa, Minnesota, Nebraska, Kansas, and Colorado.
