## METHODOLOGY

### Composite Score Calculation (0-100 scale)

| Metric | Weight | Scoring |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Overall CMS Rating | 25% | (rating/5) * 25 |
| Health Inspection | 20% | (rating/5) * 20 |
| Staffing Rating | 15% | (rating/5) * 15 |
| Quality Measures | 15% | (rating/5) * 15 |
| Staff Turnover | 15% | ((100-turnover)/100) * 15 |
| Penalty-Free | 10% | 10 if no penalties, 0 if penalties |

**Higher score = Better performing facility**
